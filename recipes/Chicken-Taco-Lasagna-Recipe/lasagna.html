Chicken Taco Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for chicken taco lasagna. It is a twist on the classic lasagna recipe

It will take 1 hour and 15 minutes to cook. 20 minutes to prep. And will serve 8 people.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 2 packets taco seasoning mix
  • 3/2 cups shredded mozarella cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup salsa
  • salt and ground pepper to taste
  • 1 package no-boil lasagna noodles

steps

  1. Combine chicken, lime and taco seasoning
  2. Cook chicken mixture over medium heat for 15 minutes
  3. mix mozarella cheese, sour cream, and salsa. Add salt and pepper
  4. put a few spoonfuls of the mixture on a baking dish
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until noodles are tender and cheese has melted
